SIGMET Plotter

SIGMET Plotter is an aviation weather visualization tool designed for pilots and dispatchers.

The app parses raw SIGMET text and plots hazardous weather areas directly on an interactive map. Pilots can also enter their planned route to quickly verify whether it intersects active SIGMET regions.

Key Features

  • Automatic SIGMET text parsing
  • Graphical display of weather hazard polygons
  • Route plotting and intersection check
  • Designed for fast preflight decision-making
  • Works offline after initial map load
