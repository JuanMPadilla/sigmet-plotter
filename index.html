SIGMET Plotter

SIGMET Plotter is an aviation weather visualization tool designed for pilots and dispatchers.

The app parses raw SIGMET text and plots hazardous weather areas directly on an interactive map. Pilots can also enter their planned route to quickly verify whether it intersects active SIGMET regions.

Key Features

  • Automatic SIGMET text parsing
  • Graphical display of weather hazard polygons
  • Route plotting and intersection check
  • Designed for fast preflight decision-making
  • Works offline and online

How to Use

A SIGMET (Significant Meteorological Information) is an official aviation weather advisory that warns pilots of severe weather phenomena that may affect the safety of flight.

Effective situational awareness requires two key elements: an understanding of where these hazards are located and a clear view of the intended flight route.

SIGMET PLOTTER helps you quickly and accurately identify these two critical elements.

The most important step in using the app is pasting the SIGMET information into the text field. There are two different ways to do this:

1) Plot a SIGMET

  1. Take a picture of the ACARS screen where the SIGMET information is displayed. Once the image is captured, select the text containing the coordinates that define the affected area and copy and paste it into the text field.
  2. You can also copy the SIGMET coordinates directly from the flight briefing PDF.
  3. Manually typing the coordinate sequence is possible, but it is time-consuming and prone to errors, so it is generally not recommended.
  4. Tap Plot SIGMET (or the equivalent button in your version).
  5. The hazard area will appear as a shaded polygon on the map.

2) Plot Your Route

  1. Enter your route (waypoints / coordinates) in the Route input field.
  2. Tap Plot Route.
  3. The route will be drawn on the map with pins at each waypoint.

3) Check for Intersection

  1. With both the SIGMET area and the route displayed, visually verify whether the route crosses or approaches the SIGMET polygon.
  2. If needed, clear and re-plot after editing the SIGMET or route text.

4) Clear the Map

  1. Tap Clear SIGMET to remove the hazard area.
  2. Tap Clear Route to remove the route and waypoint pins.
  3. Use Reset (if available) to clear everything and start fresh.
Tip: For best offline use, open the app on Wi-Fi before a flight so map tiles are cached.
Important: This app is for situational awareness and training purposes only. Always use official weather briefings, charts, and company procedures for operational decisions.
