SIGMET Plotter is an aviation weather visualization tool designed for pilots and dispatchers.

The app parses raw SIGMET text and plots hazardous weather areas directly on an interactive map. Pilots can also enter their planned route to quickly verify whether it intersects active SIGMET regions.

Key Features

  • Automatic SIGMET text parsing
  • Graphical display of weather hazard polygons
  • Route plotting and intersection check
  • Designed for fast preflight decision-making
  • Works offline and online

How to Use

A SIGMET (Significant Meteorological Information) is an official aviation weather advisory warning pilots of severe weather phenomena that may affect the safety of flight.

Effective situational awareness requires two key elements: understanding where hazards are located and clearly visualizing your intended flight route.

SIGMET Plotter helps you quickly identify these two critical elements.

1) Plot a SIGMET

  1. Take a picture of the ACARS screen showing the SIGMET information.
  2. Select and copy the coordinates defining the affected area.
  3. Paste them into the app‚Äôs text field.
  4. Tap Plot SIGMET.
  5. The hazard area appears as a shaded polygon on the map.

2) Plot Your Route

  1. Enter your planned route (waypoints or coordinates).
  2. Tap Plot Route.
  3. The route is displayed with waypoint markers.

3) Clear the Map

  1. Tap Clear SIGMET to remove the hazard area.
  2. Tap Clear Route to remove the route and pins.
  3. Use Reset to clear everything and start fresh.
Tip: For best offline performance, open the app on Wi-Fi before a flight so map tiles are cached.
Important: This app is for situational awareness and training purposes only. Always rely on official weather briefings and company procedures for operational decisions.
